## ğŸ“± Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Helpful** but not condescending
- **Clear** but not oversimplified
- **Professional** but approachable

### Language Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use active voice: "We verify" not "Verification is done"
- Be direct: "This is false" not "This might not be true"
- Show evidence: "According to BBC..." not "Sources say..."
- Empower users: "You can verify" not "Trust us"

**Don't:**
- Claim 100% accuracy
- Use fear-mongering language
- Oversimplify complex issues
- Make absolute statements without evidence
